Built for modern security teams

Everything you need for secure, auditable, and automated secrets management with cryptographic verifiability.

Ephemeral Secrets & Secure Vaults

Keyorix uses short-lived, verifiable secrets that automatically expire and rotate to enable true zero trust. Secure vaults and ephemeral credentials power Web3, ephemeral compute, DevSecOps, edge nodes, and IoT devices, ensuring secrets exist only as long as needed.

Cryptographic Verifiability

All access actions are secured with cryptographic proofs and zero-knowledge protocols. Every interaction is verifiable without exposing sensitive data, providing trust and full accountability across cloud, on-prem, edge, and IoT systems.

Multi-Environment Integration

Unified integration across cloud, on-prem, Kubernetes, edge, and IoT environments. Manage secrets, access, and policies consistently across all infrastructures, enabling secure and verifiable operations wherever workloads run.

Developer-Friendly API & CLI

REST, GraphQL, and CLI interfaces with SDKs for Python, Go, JavaScript, and more. Integrate secrets and policies directly into your stack, including edge and IoT applications.

Zero Trust Architecture

Enforces a Zero Trust model across all layers — identities, secrets, and workloads are never implicitly trusted. Short-lived credentials and cryptographic proofs continuously verify access across cloud, on-prem, edge, and IoT nodes.

Compliance & Standards

EU Compliance-by-Design: NIS2, DORA, AI Act, GDPR, ENS. Built-in controls, encryption, audit trails, and data residency ensure traceability, resilience, and regulatory alignment across all environments.

Open-Core & Extensible Plugin Architecture

Keyorix is open-core with enterprise features. A modular plugin system allows the community and third-party developers to extend the platform with custom integrations, new features, and workflows — adapting to diverse use cases in cloud, on-prem, edge, and IoT.

AI-Powered Security Assistant

Integrated AI assistant analyzes patterns of secret usage, access behavior, and infrastructure configurations — without accessing actual secrets. Detects anomalies, predicts risks, and recommends best practices in real time. Context-aware and integrated with Git, CI/CD, Kubernetes, and edge/IoT environments.

Edge & IoT Ready

Keyorix extends zero-trust, ephemeral secrets, and cryptographic controls to edge nodes and IoT devices, ensuring secure, verifiable access and automated workflows across distributed, low-trust environments.

Secure Key Architecture

Transparent, auditable, and ephemeral key management across cloud, on-prem, edge, and IoT environments. Keys are cryptographically verifiable, short-lived, and automatically rotated. Integrated with AI Assistant for real-time pattern analysis, Web3 smart contracts, cloud services, and modular plugins — enforcing Zero Trust everywhere.
